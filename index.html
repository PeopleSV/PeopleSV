About

Silicon Valley is impossible to be replicated.
Its essence still can be learned from SV Experts

People @ SV is a leading solution provider for Human Resources management. We focus on cross boarder talent acquisition, short term overseas’ assignment and sending experts to emerging markets. Based in Silicon Valley, the world’s center of innovation, we leverage the newest technology to create unique, interactive and productive recruitment experiences for companies both online and offline. Your needs are our best interests. We not only provide quality solutions but also strive to be your trusted strategic partner.

The Mission

Mission of People @ SiliconValley is to Inspire Knowledge Sharing. We focus on three essences that Silicon Valley does the best in the world: Innovation, Talent Development and Technology. Our starting points are Silicon Valley and China, our final goal is far beyond the two regions
